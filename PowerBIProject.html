Power BI Projects

Visualizing customer retention and diversity analysis for better business insights.

Customer Retention Analysis

In this project, I created a Power BI dashboard for a telecom company's retention manager, visualizing customer churn and retention data. The analysis helped identify customers at risk of leaving, enabling proactive measures to retain them. The dashboard provided actionable insights through interactive visuals, demonstrating a clear understanding of customer behaviors and trends.

Diversity and Inclusion Analytics

Working with HR data from the telecom sector, I created a Power BI dashboard focusing on gender balance and diversity in the executive management level. Key performance indicators (KPIs) such as gender distribution, promotion rates, and turnover were analyzed, providing clear insights into the diversity and inclusion efforts within the company. The dashboard helped management understand areas of improvement and track progress.

Key Skills Demonstrated

  • Data Analysis & Visualization using Power BI
  • Customer Retention Strategy Development
  • Diversity & Inclusion Analytics
  • Business Intelligence and Reporting

In these projects, I leveraged Power BI to transform raw data into insightful visuals that drive business decisions. The dashboards enable easy interpretation of complex data, supporting client objectives in retention and diversity initiatives.
